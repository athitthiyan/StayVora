# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in Stayvora a respectful, welcoming, and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, patient, and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions before escalating disagreement.
- Keep feedback focused on the work and its impact.
- Respect maintainers' time by providing clear reproduction steps and focused pull requests.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or repeated unwelcome attention.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Disruptive behavior that makes collaboration harder for others.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues or pull requests, or block participants when behavior violates this code of conduct. Serious or repeated violations can result in a permanent ban from project spaces.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer through GitHub or email support@stayvora.co.in. Reports will be reviewed with care and handled as confidentially as practical.
